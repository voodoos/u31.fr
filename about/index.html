Who ?

I am a (soon former) PhD student in computer science. My main research topics involve logic, proof-search, functional programming and language design. I am also a documentation addict and take an unnatural pleasure in learning new languages, technologies and tools. This website itself is written in OCaml/ReasonML, compiled to ReactJS and statically built with GatsbyJS...

Why is this blog Naked ?

I would like this website to be more than a simple "code blog" with programming tips and so on. It aims to be a personal place where I will also share non-computer related projects and thoughts about books and movies I enjoy.

The Naked Lunch is both of these: a science-fiction novel written by William S. Burroughs, and a movie by David Cronenberg inspired by the book.

Sorry, you will not find nudes on this blog, apart from some obscene origamis...
